The service, as-is

ARD is provided free of charge, with no guarantee of uptime, availability, or continued operation. Road-condition reports are submitted by other players and the software that watches their game client — they are best-effort community reports, not verified fact. A "clear" report can be wrong; a hazard report can be stale or mistaken. Do not treat anything shown on this map as a guarantee of safety, and don't rely on it as your only source of judgment when traveling — this is a convenience tool, not a safety system.
